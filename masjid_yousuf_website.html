Welcome to Our Community

Masjid Yousuf serves as a spiritual home for the Muslim community in Forest Gate, East London. We are dedicated to providing a welcoming space for prayer, education, and community engagement.

Our masjid offers daily prayers, Jumu'ah congregational prayers, Islamic education for all ages, and a range of community services designed to support and strengthen our local neighbourhood.

Our Services

Programs and facilities available for the community

📖

Qur'an Classes

Structured Qur'an recitation and memorization classes for children and adults, taught by qualified instructors in a supportive environment.

🎓

Islamic Education

Comprehensive Islamic studies covering fiqh, hadith, seerah, and aqeedah for all age groups, from beginners to advanced learners.

🤝

Community Support

Counselling services, marriage support, funeral arrangements, and welfare assistance for community members in need.

👶

Youth Programs

Engaging activities, sports, mentorship, and leadership development programs designed specifically for young Muslims.

🍲

Community Kitchen

Regular community meals, iftar gatherings during Ramadan, and food bank services to support those facing hardship.

💒

Nikah Services

Marriage solemnization services, pre-marital counselling, and wedding facilitation in accordance with Islamic principles.
